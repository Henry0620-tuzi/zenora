Customer Support

Fit Guidance US + Europe Delivery Confident Checkout

Answers that make a premium cross-border purchase feel more certain.

For a women-led fashion brand selling into the United States and Europe, strong FAQ content does real conversion work. It answers fit concerns, shipping expectations, customs uncertainty, and return anxiety before those objections interrupt an otherwise high-intent purchase.

Sizing

How do I choose my size with confidence?

Start with the measurements listed on each product page. If you are between sizes, follow the fit note shown under the size selector. Most ZENORA styles are designed to fit true to size with a clean, softly relaxed line rather than a body-con fit.

Model information is included wherever possible to give extra context on height and size worn.

Shipping

How long does delivery take from order to arrival?

Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days. Standard delivery to the United States is estimated at 6-10 business days, while many European destinations can be positioned around 4-8 business days depending on the final routing setup.

If expedited delivery is offered, those options should appear during checkout once your logistics setup is connected.

Returns

Can I return or exchange an item if the fit is not right?

Returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery on unworn items with tags attached. Exchange policies can be added once your operational workflow is clear, but many growing brands begin with returns only for simplicity across regions.

Final sale products should be clearly labeled and excluded from return eligibility.

Duties & Taxes

Will I need to pay customs duties or import taxes?

Duties and taxes depend on the destination country and your checkout configuration. ZENORA should surface this clearly at checkout so US and European customers understand total landed cost before placing the order.

Care

How should I care for ZENORA garments to preserve the shape?

Each product page should list exact care instructions. In general, delicate cycle or cold hand wash, low-heat steaming, and line drying help preserve the cleaner lines and softer premium textures that define the brand.

Contact

How can I reach customer support if I need help before ordering?

You can position support around email first during early launch. Example: support@zenora.com. A realistic promise is to respond within 24-48 business hours for sizing, shipping, and order questions.

You can also route wholesale, creator collaboration, and press inquiries through separate inboxes once the brand grows.
