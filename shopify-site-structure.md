## 二、核心页面清单

### 1. 首页 Home

作用：建立品牌感、快速展示爆款和利益点、把访客导向集合页或商品页。

推荐模块：

- Announcement Bar
- Hero Banner
- Category Grid
- Best Sellers
- New Arrivals
- Brand Story
- Trust Badges
- Customer Reviews
- Instagram / UGC
- Email Signup

首页首屏文案示例：

- Headline: Modern Essentials for Everyday Confidence
- Subheadline: Designed for movement, made for repeat wear, and shipped worldwide.
- CTA 1: Shop New In
- CTA 2: Explore Best Sellers

利益点文案示例：

- Premium fabrics that hold shape and feel soft all day
- Thoughtful fits with easy-to-read size guidance
- Fast worldwide shipping on selected regions
- Easy returns for a more confident first order

### 2. 集合页 Collection Page

作用：帮助用户快速筛选风格、尺码和价格带。

必备元素：

- 清晰集合标题
- 简短集合描述
- 筛选器：Size / Color / Price / Availability
- 排序器：Featured / Best Selling / Newest / Price
- 商品卡片卖点标签

集合页标题示例：

- New In
- Summer Dresses
- Tailored Sets
- Weekend Essentials

集合页描述示例：

- Fresh arrivals selected for everyday wear, weekend dressing, and effortless layering.

### 3. 商品页 Product Page

作用：完成转化，是服装站最关键页面。

推荐结构：

- 商品标题
- 价格与折扣
- 颜色选择
- 尺码选择
- 尺码推荐文案
- 加购按钮
- 分期或支付信任提示
- 核心卖点摘要
- 图文详情
- 面料与护理
- 尺码说明
- 物流与退换货
- 搭配推荐
- 用户评价

商品页标题示例：

- The Aria Linen Blend Dress
- The Sloane Relaxed Shirt
- The Mira Tailored Wide-Leg Pant

商品页摘要模板：

- Soft structure, breathable feel, and a flattering silhouette for everyday wear.
- Designed to pair easily with your wardrobe staples from weekday to weekend.

商品页卖点模板：

- Lightweight and breathable fabric
- Easy fit with clean lines
- Fully lined or non-sheer where applicable
- Designed for repeat wear and simple styling

尺码区文案模板：

- Between sizes? Choose the larger size for a more relaxed fit.
- Model is 177 cm / 5'10" and wears size S.

物流文案模板：

- Processing time: 1-3 business days
- Estimated delivery: 6-10 business days for the US
- Duties and taxes calculated based on destination at checkout if applicable

退换货文案模板：

- Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery on unworn items with tags attached.

### 4. About Us

作用：提升品牌可信度，解释品牌为什么存在。

文案结构：

- 品牌起点
- 设计理念
- 面料/版型哲学
- 对客户承诺

文案示例：

- We created the brand for women who want polished pieces that still feel easy to wear.
- Our collections focus on clean shapes, versatile colors, and fabrics chosen for comfort in daily life.

### 5. FAQ

建议问题：

- How do I choose my size?
- Do you ship internationally?
- How long does shipping take?
- Can I return or exchange an item?
- Will I need to pay customs duties?
- How should I care for this garment?

### 6. Size Guide

内容建议：

- International size conversion
- Body measurement guide
- How to measure bust / waist / hips
- Fit notes by category

### 7. Contact Us

内容建议：

- Contact form
- Support email
- Response time promise
- Order issues / wholesale / influencer inquiries

## 四、商品页文案模板

你可以给每个商品套这个结构：

### 标题

- [Product Name]

### 一句话摘要

- A clean, easy-to-style piece designed for comfort, movement, and repeat wear.

### 卖点

- Soft hand feel
- Breathable fabric
- Flattering shape
- Versatile styling

### 详情段落

- [Product Name] is designed to bring ease to everyday dressing. The silhouette feels polished without being restrictive, while the fabric offers comfort for long wear. Pair it with sandals, sneakers, or a tailored layer depending on the season.

### 面料和护理

- Fabric: [xx% cotton / xx% linen / xx% polyester]
- Care: Cold wash or hand wash recommended. Hang dry. Do not bleach.

### 尺码建议

- True to size
- If you prefer a looser fit, size up
- Model is [height] and wears size [S/M]
